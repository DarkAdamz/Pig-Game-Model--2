Rules of the Game

This are the rules of the game:

  • Each player will have a current score and a global score
  • The first person to reach the goal wins the goal is usually 100.
  • You have the option of holding the values of the dices and passing the dices to the next person
  • If you roll a one on any of the dice, you will lose your current score and the dices will be passed to the next player
  • If you understand the rules click PLAY and may the best man win. GOODLUCK!!
